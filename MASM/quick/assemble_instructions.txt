To assemble and link:
    ml64.exe main.asm v_asc.asm v_bin1.asm v_hex1.asm /link /SUBSYSTEM:console /ENTRY:main

To assemble, build a lib and link (must specify includelib v.lib in main.asm):
    ml64.exe /c main.asm v_asc.asm v_bin1.asm v_hex1.asm
    lib.exe /out:v.lib /verbose v_asc.obj v_bin1.obj v_hex1.obj
    ml64.exe main.asm /link /SUBSYSTEM:console /ENTRY:main

Single asm file:
    ml64.exe main.asm /link /SUBSYSTEM:console /ENTRY:main
